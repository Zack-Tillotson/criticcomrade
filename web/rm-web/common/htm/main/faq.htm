Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is Critic Comrade?
Critic Comrade is a movie review site that gives personalized suggestions on movies.
Why is Critic Comrade better?
Critic Comrade gives you personal suggestions on whether you will like a movie or not. Add to that the fact that Critic Comrade has the most reviews and is the most fun and it can't be beat :)

Movies

What is a CCCP suggestion?
This is the personal suggestion Critic Comrade offers based on your taste in movies. (CCCP Suggestion stands for CriticComrade.Com Personalized Suggestion).
Why should I rate more movies?
Because Critic Comrade bases its suggestions on the movies you've rated previously, if you don't have enough reviews the suggestions won't be accurate. We've found that 15 reviews is a good minimum for accurate suggestions (but of course it's still useful with fewer than that).

Critics

What are your best critics?
These are the critics you agree with the most. They are also referred to on this site as your "Comrades", and they are the critics who have the most agreeing reviews with you.
What is the "agreeing reviews" score for a critic?
This is the number of reviews that you and the critic agree on. So if you review 20 movies, and the critic reviews 100 movies, then you might have both reviewed 10 of the same movies. Of those 10 that you both reviewed, the more that you agree on the better.
